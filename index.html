About Vijayanand Trust

Social Psychological Educational Medical Research Rehabilitation

Vijayanand Trust works in Social, Psychological, Educational, Medical, Research and Rehabilitation fields. We believe in improving mental, physical and emotional well-being of society.

Our Focus

Child development
Youth development
Women empowerment
Old age support
Marriage guidance (Vadhu–Var Suchak Kendra)
Career guidance
Personality development

Vision

To achieve behavioural, social, educational and economic transformation for all.

Mission

To use best resources to solve problems of normal and special people and improve quality of life.

Goal

To improve access and quality of life for everyone.
