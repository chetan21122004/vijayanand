About Vijayanand Trust

Social Psychological Educational Medical Research Rehabilitation

Our trust is work on Social, Psychological, Educational, Medical, Research and Rehabilitation for all. Everyone have Well-being of mental, physical and economical domain, Our society is representing to diversity and diversity need to equality of thought, then we can achieve to destination of Life.

Our Focus

“VIJAYANAND TRUST” focuses on child development, to provide Education for Young, Support to women for independent in life, caring to old age people, to help you for choice Marriage life partner (Vadhu - Var Suchak Kendra), assess your child ability, aptitude and choice his/ her carrier, some courses also you can complete under “PACE INSTITUTE”.

Vision

We aim to achieve Behavioural, Social, Educational and Economic transformation for all towards Rehabilitation. All round development of children, Young, Adult, and old age people and give opportunity to all for succeed.

Mission

Use optimum level of resources in society for solution of the problems of all Persons with Special need as well as Normal.

Goal

Our goal is to improve access and quality of life.
