About Vijayanand Foundation

Our trust works in Social, Psychological, Educational, Medical, Research and Rehabilitation fields.

Everyone has well-being of mental, physical and economical domain. Our society represents diversity and we need equality of thought to achieve a better life.

Vijayanand Foundation focuses on:

Child development
Education for youth
Support to women for independent life
Care of old age people
Marriage guidance (Vadhu–Var Suchak Kendra)
Child ability and aptitude assessment
Career guidance
Personality development
Courses under PACE Institute
Our Specialized Centre

Vijayanand Child Counseling and Therapy Centre

Empowering lives, one step at a time.

Come and see the difference compassionate care can make.

Vision

We aim to achieve behavioural, social, educational and economic transformation for all towards rehabilitation. All round development of children, young, adult and old age people and give opportunity to all for success.

Mission

Use optimum level of resources in society for solution of the problems of all persons with special need as well as normal.

Goal

Our goal is to improve access and quality of life.
