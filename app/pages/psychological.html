Counselling of School Children

School children problems are increasing day by day. They cannot pay attention during childhood period. They may be suffering from challenges and difficulties.

If we identify and prevent problems at early stage, we can stop big problems.

Common problems:

  • • Learning problem
  • • Reading problem
  • • Writing problem
  • • Lack of self-esteem
  • • Lack of motivation
  • • Orientation problem
  • • Self-confidence problem
  • • Phobia
  • • Anxiety
  • • Stress
  • • Maladaptive behaviour
